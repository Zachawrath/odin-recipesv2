Buiscuit Burger Bomb

Home
  • 1 can of butter buiscuits
  • 1 pickle per buiscuit
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • burger seasoning
  • shredded cheese
  • 2 tbps butter
  1. pre-heat oven to 375 degrees
  2. cook and season ground beef
  3. flatten buiscuit place 1 pickle and a light amount of meat in the center of the buiscuit
  4. shape the buiscuit around meat and pickle like a dumpling
  5. add butter and shredded cheese on top
  6. cook for 15 minutes
